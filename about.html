How It Started
Built out of frustration

We started Placeholder in 2022 after one too many bad experiences with overpriced tools that promised the world and delivered a spreadsheet.

Working from a small office in Wellington, we decided to strip everything back — no fluff, no unnecessary complexity. Just a product that works, backed by people who actually care.

Today we work with clients across New Zealand, Australia, and beyond. Still just two of us. Still refusing to scale for the sake of it.

What We Believe
Our values

A small team runs on strong principles. These are the ones we actually live by.

Honesty over hype

We tell you what we can actually do, not what sounds good in a pitch deck. No buzzwords, no inflated promises — just clear communication and real outcomes.

Quality over quantity

We deliberately keep our client list small so every project gets our full attention. You're not a ticket in a queue — you're working directly with the people building your product.

Move fast, stay lean

Two people means zero bureaucracy. We make decisions quickly, ship often, and iterate based on real feedback — not committee meetings and quarterly planning cycles.

People first

We started this because we wanted to work with good people on interesting problems. That hasn't changed. Every relationship matters and every client gets treated like a long-term partner.

Radical transparency

You'll always know where your project stands. We share progress openly, flag problems early, and never hide behind corporate speak when something isn't working.

Built to last

We don't take shortcuts. Every product we build is architected to scale, designed to be maintained, and documented so you're never left holding something only we understand.
