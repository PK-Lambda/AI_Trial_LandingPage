Overview
What is Service One?

Service One was built for teams who are tired of duct-taped solutions. We take a structured, end-to-end approach that covers everything from initial scoping through to delivery and ongoing support.

Whether you're starting from scratch or improving an existing process, Service One adapts to where you are and scales as you grow. We've refined this service across dozens of engagements to make it genuinely repeatable and reliable.

How it works
Our process

A clear, repeatable process that keeps you informed at every step.

1

Discovery

We start with a deep-dive session to understand your goals, constraints, and current setup. No assumptions — just good questions.

2

Scoping & Proposal

We turn what we learned into a clear scope of work with defined deliverables, timeline, and pricing. You approve before anything begins.

3

Delivery

We build, configure, or implement — depending on the nature of the engagement — with regular check-ins so you're always in the loop.

4

Review & Handover

We walk you through everything we've delivered, make sure it meets your expectations, and hand over all relevant documentation.

5

Support

For a defined period after handover, we're available to answer questions, fix issues, and make minor adjustments at no extra cost.
